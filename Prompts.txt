Variant: Handheld Eye-Level Inspection Shot



Prompt:

A realistic close-up handheld smartphone photograph of a prominent concrete crack stretching across a building's load-bearing exterior wall, eye-level perspective. The shot is taken from a human standing distance, capturing true-to-life structural damage. The vertical cement wall surface is unpolished and weathered, showing grit, micro-fractures branching off the main fault, staining from rain runoff, and a rough masonry texture. The primary crack is uneven and jagged, cutting deeply into the substrate with visible depth. Natural overcast daylighting creates soft, realistic shadows inside the fracture line. Sharp focus, clear details on stone grains, documentary style.

Prompt:

A close-up, sharp drone inspection photograph capturing a severe, long concrete crack propagating vertically up a weathered building exterior wall, structural degradation.
